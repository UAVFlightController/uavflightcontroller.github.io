About the UAV flight controller

A low-cost, versatile, autonomous Unmanned Aerial Vehicle flight controller is an electronic system, comprised of hardware and software, able to fully control any type of small fixed-wing aircraft often known as radio-controlled model airplanes.

Given the high availability and low cost of these fixed-wing aircraft, by developing a matching low-cost flight controller system, it could be possible to deploy various UAVs at a time to actively monitor high-risk areas autonomously, relaying critical information to firefighters while having few manned interventions in the surveillance process.
